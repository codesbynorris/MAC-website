About MAC Assistive Devices

MAC Assistive Devices is a platform dedicated to celebrating the strength, resilience, and achievements of individuals who use assistive devices. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential, regardless of any challenges they may face. This website is a resource hub, a community gathering place, and a source of inspiration, showcasing the diverse stories and experiences of people whose lives are enhanced by assistive technology.

Founded by Grace Kinga, author of "My Daughter, You Can Fly, Even With a Broken Wing," MAC Assistive Devices stems from a deep personal understanding of the power of support and the importance of accessible resources. Grace's book, a testament to the unwavering spirit of her daughter, serves as the guiding principle behind this website: empowering individuals to overcome obstacles and live fulfilling lives.

Our Aims

  • Showcase Stories: Share inspiring stories of individuals using assistive devices, highlighting their accomplishments, challenges, and perspectives.
  • Provide Resources: Offer valuable information and resources related to assistive devices, including where to find them, how to use them, and funding options.
  • Build Community: Create a supportive community where individuals using assistive devices, their families, and allies can connect, share experiences, and learn from each other.
  • Promote Awareness: Raise awareness about the importance of accessibility and inclusion, challenging stereotypes and fostering a more understanding and accepting society.

At MAC Assistive Devices, we believe that assistive devices are not just tools; they are keys that unlock potential, enabling individuals to participate fully in life. We are committed to empowering individuals to embrace their abilities, celebrate their uniqueness, and soar to new heights. We invite you to join us on this journey of inspiration, connection, and empowerment.
